## Getting Started

### Creating Your HostPro Account

1. Visit https://hostpro-dev-app.azurewebsites.net
2. Click "Register" or "Sign Up"
3. Enter your email and password
4. Create your property details
5. Verify your email address

### Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard shows:
- **Occupancy Rate:** Percentage of booked days
- **Monthly Revenue:** Total income this month
- **Average Price/Night:** ADR metric
- **RevPAR:** Revenue per available room
- **Upcoming Arrivals:** Next 14 days of check-ins
- **Active Properties:** Number of listed properties

## Messages

### Unified Inbox Overview

All messages from Airbnb, Booking, and Abritel appear in **one unified inbox**. No need to check multiple platforms!

### Reading Messages

1. Click **"Messages"** in the sidebar
2. Select a conversation from the list
3. Read the full message history
4. Messages automatically mark as read

### Sending Replies

**To send a reply:**

1. Click on a conversation
2. Type your response in the reply box
3. Press **Ctrl+Enter** or click **Send**
4. Your message is automatically sent to **all platforms**
5. Notification appears once sent

**Features:**
- ✅ Messages auto-save as drafts
- ✅ Character counter shows remaining space
- ✅ Ctrl+Shift+Z to undo (if draft lost)

### Searching Messages

Use the **Search box** at the top:
- Search by guest name: "Marie"
- Search by property: "Villa Azur"
- Search by message content: "WiFi"

### Filtering Messages

**Platform Filter:**
- All (shows all platforms)
- Airbnb (Airbnb only)
- Booking (Booking.com only)
- Abritel (Abritel only)

**Status Filter:**
- All
- Pending (needs response)
- Active (ongoing conversation)
- Resolved (conversation completed)
- Archived (old conversations)

### Notifications

**You'll receive notifications for:**
- New messages from guests
- Check-in/check-out times
- Task reminders
- Daily summary (optional)

**Enable Notifications:**

1. Open Messages page
2. Click "Enable Notifications" prompt
3. Allow browser notifications
4. You're set!

## Settings & Integrations

### Connecting Airbnb

1. Go to **Settings > Integrations**
2. Click **"Connect Airbnb"**
3. Log in with your Airbnb account
4. Authorize HostPro to access messages
5. Done! Messages will sync automatically

### Connecting Booking.com

1. Go to **Settings > Integrations**
2. Click **"Connect Booking"**
3. Enter your Booking.com partner ID
4. Paste your API key
5. Verify the connection
6. Messages will start syncing

### Connecting Abritel

1. Go to **Settings > Integrations**
2. Click **"Connect Abritel"**
3. Log in with your Abritel account
4. Authorize API access
5. Confirm connection
6. Messages will sync every 5 minutes

### Profile Settings

**Change Your Information:**

1. Click your name (top right)
2. Select **"My Profile"**
3. Update name, email, phone
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

### Notification Preferences

**Manage Your Notifications:**

1. Go to **Settings > Notifications**
2. Choose:
   - Push notifications (real-time)
   - Email notifications
   - Daily summary
3. Toggle on/off as preferred

### Password & Security

**Change Your Password:**

1. Go to **Settings > Security**
2. Click **"Change Password"**
3. Enter current password
4. Set new password (min 8 characters)
5. Click **"Update"**

**Active Sessions:**

1. Go to **Settings > Security**
2. See all logged-in devices
3. Click **"Sign Out"** on any device
4. Current session continues

## FAQs

### Q: How often do messages sync?
**A:** Messages sync automatically every 5 minutes. Manual sync available via "Sync Now" button.

### Q: Can I send messages to multiple platforms at once?
**A:** Yes! When you reply to a unified conversation, your message goes to all connected platforms automatically.

### Q: What if a message fails to send?
**A:** An error notification appears. Check your internet connection and try again. Contact support if issue persists.

### Q: Can I delete messages?
**A:** Messages cannot be deleted (for compliance), but conversations can be archived. Archived conversations don't appear in your main inbox.

### Q: What happens if I disconnect a platform?
**A:** You'll lose access to new messages from that platform, but past messages remain in your inbox for reference.

### Q: Is my data safe?
**A:** Yes! All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. We comply with GDPR and data protection regulations.

### Q: Do you store my API keys?
**A:** No! API keys are encrypted and only used to sync messages. We never share or misuse your credentials.

### Q: How much does HostPro cost?
**A:** HostPro is free during beta. Pricing TBA after official launch.

### Q: Can I export my messages?
**A:** Yes! Go to Settings > Data Export to download your message history as CSV or JSON.

### Q: What languages are supported?
**A:** Currently English and French. More languages coming soon.

### Q: How do I cancel my account?
**A:** Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

## Troubleshooting

### Messages not syncing?

1. Check your internet connection
2. Verify your API credentials are correct
3. Click "Sync Now" button
4. Wait 5 minutes for automatic sync
5. Contact support if issue persists

### Notifications not working?

1. Check browser allows notifications
2. Go to Settings > Notifications
3. Ensure notifications are enabled
4. Check "Do Not Disturb" is off
5. Clear browser cache and try again

### Can't log in?

1. Check email and password are correct
2. Verify Caps Lock is off
3. Try "Forgot Password?"
4. Clear browser cookies
5. Try different browser or incognito window

### Messages appearing twice?

This can happen if platforms sync overlaps. It's harmless and will be resolved in the next sync cycle.

### Slow performance?

1. Check internet speed
2. Disable browser extensions
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try different browser
5. Contact support if still slow
